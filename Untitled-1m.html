Everything you need to know about me

Nwanze Christian from Lagos,Nigeria

My name is Nwanze Christian and I am 16 years old, I just finished high school but I'm taking a gap year to learn some skills before going into the university to learn robotics engineering. I live in Lagos,Nigeria with my family. My favourite colors are blue and black or a mixture of both. During my free time, I engage myself in football, video games and creative writing.

So far with te help of the OneChildOneCare Initiative(OCOCI) in association with Tech4dev I was able to enroll in the Basic Digital Literacy programme, with the help of our facilitator allowed me to gain more knowledge in the use of the computer and in the use of the various Microsoft Offices such as:Microsoft Word, Microsoft Excel, Microsoft Powerpoint and the Outlook app(which is the e-mail app). My experience so far has being quite interesting, all thanks to the smooth communication of Ben, my facilitator and his very precise lessons. Before taking this course, i felt it would be all fun and games, however at this point, it is still fun and games but with a whole lot of slepless nights but that does not mean I would give up. I rather would work harder and put more effort into it because it is something that I love and I am passionate about.

Thank you very much for the opportunity thus far and I look forward to the end of this programme.
